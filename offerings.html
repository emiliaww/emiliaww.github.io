my offerings


Because I am beginning my practice, I am offering these discounted prices (July 29, 2020). If these prices are prohibitive, please reach out and I will work around your needs and budget. In general, I spend around an hour to two hours preparing your chart, and the session itself takes around an hour to two hours. My prices are set so that if you make minimum or low wages, you are able to afford a session. Similarly, if you make higher wages or are generationally wealthy, please pay on the higher-end of the scale so that I can continue to sustainably offer affordable services.

Natal Chart Offering (1 hr+) $40-80

    A conversation about and introduction to your birth chart, including but not limited to planets in signs and houses, aspects, lunar phase, nodes

Tarot Reading (30 mins) $10-30

Year-Ahead Reading (45mins - 1 hour) $40-80

    A conversation and introduction to transits, progressions, and annual profections for the next year, as well as incorporating your solar return chart! Best on its own if you have an active understanding of your chart or if we have worked together before!
